Italian Baked Chicken!

Description

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Mix mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, and garlic powder together in a bowl.
  3. Place bread crumbs in a separate bowl. Dip each chicken breast into the mayonnaise mixture, then into the bread crumbs to coat.
  4. Arrange breaded chicken on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the coating is golden brown, about 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
